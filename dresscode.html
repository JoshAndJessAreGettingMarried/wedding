Dress Code

There isn’t one. Truly.

Wear what makes you feel comfortable, confident, and joyful.

  • Second-hand / borrowed / re-worn outfits encouraged
  • Colourful, comfy, weird, wonderful — all welcome
  • Sensible footwear recommended for outdoor areas
  • May weather can be unpredictable — layers are your friend

No suits required. No heels expected. No rules.
